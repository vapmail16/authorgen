# Minimal White Theme

- **Background**: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- **Text**: Dark gray (#2C2C2C)
- **Accent**: Soft blue (#4A90E2)
- **Borders**: Light gray (#F0F0F0)

## Typography
- **Primary**: 'Inter', sans-serif
- **Display**: 'Playfair Display', serif

## Key Features
- Ultra minimal design
- Lots of white space
- Thin borders
- Subtle shadows
- Clean, professional look
